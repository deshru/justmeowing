Step 01
Post Your Project

Tell us what you need — describe the project, set a budget, and choose a deadline. Our smart matching algorithm instantly connects you with the best-fit freelancers for your needs.

Pro tip: The more detail you provide, the better your proposals will be. Include examples, files, or references.
💬
Step 02
Review Proposals

Within minutes, verified freelancers send tailored proposals. Browse portfolios, read reviews, compare prices, and chat to find your perfect match.

Pro tip: Look at portfolio samples and response speed — these are great quality indicators.
🚀
Step 03
Work Together

Once hired, collaborate through our workspace. Set milestones, share files, track progress, and communicate in real time — all in one place.

Pro tip: Break large projects into milestones so both sides stay aligned throughout.
✅
Step 04
Pay Securely

Funds are held securely in escrow. Release payment milestone by milestone, only when you're satisfied. Full money-back guarantee on every project.

Pro tip: Use milestone payments for long projects to maintain quality control.
Got Questions?
Frequently asked
How does payment protection work?
▼
Your payment is held securely in escrow and only released to the freelancer once you approve the work. If you're not satisfied, you're protected by our money-back guarantee.
How are freelancers vetted?
▼
Every freelancer goes through identity verification, portfolio review, and skill assessments. Only the top 15% of applicants pass our vetting process.
What fees does Skillr charge?
▼
Clients pay a flat 5% service fee. Freelancers pay 10% on their first $500 with a client, then just 5% thereafter — one of the lowest rates in the industry.
How fast can I get started?
▼
You can post a project and start receiving proposals within minutes. Most clients receive 5–10 qualified proposals within the first 2 hours.
Can I hire someone full-time?
▼
Yes! Our Enterprise plan allows you to hire freelancers on a monthly retainer or transition them to a full-time contract at any time.
What if the work isn't what I expected?
▼
Request revisions directly through the platform. If disputes arise, our Resolution Center mediates and ensures a fair outcome for both parties.
